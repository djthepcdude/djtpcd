Recycle or Donate Computer Parts

Support local tech reuse. Good parts are reused first, then responsibly recycled.

Accepted

  • GPUs (modern generation)
  • DDR4/DDR5 RAM
  • SSDs and NVMe drives
  • 80+ rated power supplies
  • Recent motherboards and coolers

Not Accepted

  • Broken batteries or leaking components
  • Damaged moldy hardware
  • Obsolete storage media (floppy/IDE)
